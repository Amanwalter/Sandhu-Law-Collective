ABOUT US

Sandhu Law Collective

Sandhu Law Collective (SLC) is a boutique litigation and advisory firm founded in 2014 to provide prosecution, advisory, litigation and enforcement services across diverse areas of law.

Based in the Chandigarh Capital Region, the firm’s core practice centres around the Hon’ble Punjab and Haryana High Court, while also appearing before the Hon’ble Supreme Court of India, various High Courts, Tribunals, District Courts and statutory authorities.
